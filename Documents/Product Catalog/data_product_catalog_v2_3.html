Accrual Income & GSA Revenue Tracker

Why This Product Matters

DP1 tracks expenses (what GSA spends on contractors). DP4 tracks income (what client agencies owe GSA for services). Without DP4, the CFO cannot produce a complete P&L or balance sheet for the revolving fund. Income recognition at USSGL 5000-level is mandated by FASAB SFFAS 7 (Revenue). LOA-level distribution enables attribution of revenue back to individual Treasury accounts. DP4 also enriches DP1's accrued_amt column via the CDC pipeline — creating the only bidirectional dependency in the architecture.

DATA PRODUCT 5

Treasury Transmission & Payment Pipeline

Why This Product Matters

Every invoice processed in ASSIST must be transmitted to Pegasys for payment. Pegasys returns a receiving report (RR) confirming delivery, and VITAP processes the final disbursement. Transmit errors, suspension windows, and reconciliation gaps between bill_item amounts and transmitted amounts are major audit findings. The Prompt Payment Act (31 U.S.C. §3901) requires payment within 30 days — this product enables SLA monitoring per invoice across all Treasury-bound transmissions.

DATA PRODUCT 7

Solicitation & Market Intelligence

Why This Product Matters

Current DPs capture what was awarded. DP7 captures what was competed — including losing bids. This is the basis for price reasonableness determinations (FAR 15.404), analysis of how many vendors actually compete on each vehicle, and whether direct-connect vs. open postings yield different bid counts. The solicit_response table contains losing bid amounts, critical for should-cost benchmarking in future acquisitions.

Primary Consumers

Obligation accounting. Drives committed → certified → obligated → expended progression in fact_obligation_expenditure (DP1).

Revenue Recognition

Accrual income recognition at USSGL 5000-level. Implemented in DP4 (Accrual Income Reporter) and enriched back to DP1.accrued_amt.

UDO Review Requirements

Annual UDO review. v_udo_aging (DP1) implements aging buckets (Active, <1yr, 1-2yr, >2yr) required for OMB submission.

No Obligation Beyond Appropriations

LOA-level grain + TAS on dim_loa ensures every obligation is traceable to a specific appropriation and fiscal year (DP1, DP9).

Invoice-to-payment SLA monitoring. DP5 Treasury Pipeline tracks Pegasys transmission lag per invoice.

v_fpds_usaspending_extract (DP3) columns named per DAIMS v2.0 schema for API push to USASpending.gov.
